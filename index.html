Our Stroy

Founded by two high school friends in 2016, For Better or Wurst took advantage of the food truck craze and started a German Food truck. With their combined background of self taught cooking skills, MBA, and German heritage, the duo started selling their wares at local farmers markets and catering events such as Ocktoberfeast and private parties before graduating to a food truck in 2016. Since then For Better or Wurst has been met with great praise and was the winner of the Best Food Truck in Knoxville, and has been featured on shows such as Diners Drive thru’s and Dives, and was runner up in 2015’s The Great Food Truck Race.
